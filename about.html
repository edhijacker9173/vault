About The Vault

A digital cabinet of curiosities.

Built one obsession at a time: strange histories, literary universes, timelines, maps, shelves, and reference tools that are more useful when they can be explored rather than simply read.

Why it exists

For the subjects that refuse to stay in one neat box.

The Vault is where I collect the rabbit holes I keep coming back to: authors, expeditions, orders, myths, chronologies, and the little connective tissue that makes a topic feel alive.

Each collection is designed as a small working archive. Some are shelves, some are maps, some are timelines, and some are experiments in how to make dense material easier to scan, compare, and revisit.
